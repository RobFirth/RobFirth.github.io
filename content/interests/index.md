I co-initiated the [Intelligent Observatory](https://www.ukri.org/news/uk-south-africa-partnership-uses-ai-to-make-telescopes-smarter/) programme with the South African Astronomical Observatory, applying AI to automate and improve telescope operations.

<div id="astrophysics" class="interest-block">

## Astrophysics

Before AI research, I studied Type Ia supernovae — thermonuclear explosions used as distance markers in cosmology. My PhD at Southampton focused on early-time light curves and what they tell us about how these stars explode.

</div>
